#blackjack

Rules !!!

  1. Player places a bet
  2. Dealer deals cards to player
    • Player cards: both open
    • Dealer cards: one card closed and the other open
  3. Player decides how to play hand a.
    • A player can choose to “hit” and request an additional card from the deck.
    • A player can choose to “stand” and keep the original cards dealt.
  4. Dealer plays hand (shows his closed card)
  5. Payouts
